# Types of Internet Connections

## Broadband
High-speed internet connection using DSL or cable.

Advantages
- Faster than dial-up
- Always connected

Disadvantages
- Speed depends on provider

---

## Fiber Optic

Uses light signals through fiber cables.

Advantages
- Very high speed
- Reliable

Disadvantages
- Expensive installation

---

## Satellite Internet

Internet provided through satellites.

Advantages
- Available in remote areas

Disadvantages
- High latency
- Expensive
